Description

Eddy is diagramming software for the Graphol language written in python. Graphol is developed by members of the DASI-lab group of the [Dipartimento di Ingegneria Informatica, Automatica e Gestionale "A.Ruberti"] at Sapienza University of Rome. Graphol offers a completely visual representation of ontologies to users, in order to help understanding by people who are not skilled in logic. Graphol provides designers with simple graphical primitives for ontology editing, avoiding complex textual syntax. Graphol's basic components are inspired by Entity Relationship (ER) diagrams, thus ontologies that can be rendered as ER diagrams have in Graphol a similar diagrammatic shape.
